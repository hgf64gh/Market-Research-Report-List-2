<p><p>The Volume Bragg Grating market comprises several types: Reflective Bragg Gratings (RBG), which reflect specific wavelengths of light; Transmission Bragg Gratings (TBG), allowing light to pass through while reflecting certain wavelengths; Color-selective Bragg Gratings (CBG), which filter colors selectively; and Broadband Notch Filters (BNF), designed to block a wide range of wavelengths while transmitting others. These technologies find applications in telecommunications, optical components, and sensor systems, enhancing performance and functionality in various industries.</p></p>
