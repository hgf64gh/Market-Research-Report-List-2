<p><p>X-ray inspection machines encompass various technologies, primarily Digital Radiography (DR) and Computed Tomography (CT). Digital Radiography utilizes digital sensors to capture images, offering faster processing and enhanced image quality for real-time analysis. Computed Tomography provides cross-sectional images, allowing for detailed visualization of structures, crucial in complex inspections. Other types include traditional film-based systems and newer innovations like portable X-ray units, each serving specific applications across industries such as healthcare, manufacturing, and security.</p></p>
