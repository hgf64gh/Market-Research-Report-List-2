<p><ul><li>Die Casting</li><li>Permanent Mold Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum castings market encompasses various casting methods, primarily die casting and permanent mold casting. Die casting involves forcing molten aluminum into a mold under high pressure, ensuring precision and smooth finishes, making it ideal for large production volumes. In contrast, permanent mold casting uses reusable molds, allowing for better surface quality and dimensional accuracy, suitable for medium production runs. Both methods cater to diverse industries, including automotive and aerospace, highlighting their significance in producing lightweight, durable components.</p></p>
