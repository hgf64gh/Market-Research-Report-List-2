Statistical arbitrage funds leverage mathematical models to find pricing discrepancies among securities. Convertible arbitrage focuses on mispricing between convertible securities and underlying stocks, while fixed income arbitrage involves exploiting yield differentials in bonds. Commodity spread trades involve profiting from price differences in related commodities, encompassing a range of systematic trading approaches.</p></p>
