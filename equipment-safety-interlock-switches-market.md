<p><ul><li>Magnetic-Force Interlocking</li><li>Spring-Force Interlocking</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Equipment safety interlock switches are essential for ensuring operational safety in various industrial applications. The magnetic-force interlocking type uses magnets to securely lock equipment, ensuring that it cannot be operated when safety guards are open, thus preventing accidents. Conversely, the spring-force interlocking mechanism relies on springs to maintain a locked position until the appropriate safety conditions are met, ensuring that equipment remains inoperable during hazardous situations. Both types enhance workplace safety by minimizing the risk of unintended equipment activation.</p></p>
