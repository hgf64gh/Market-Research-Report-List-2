<p><p>The cordless electric string trimmer market is primarily divided into two types: battery-powered and charger power supply models. Conversely, charger power supply trimmers require a constant connection to a power source, offering continuous operation without battery limitations. This distinction highlights the different consumer needs, with battery-powered models favoring convenience and mobility, while charger-powered options cater to more extensive, uninterrupted trimming tasks.</p></p>
