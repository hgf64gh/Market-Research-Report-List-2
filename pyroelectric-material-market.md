<p><strong>The Pyroelectric Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ferroelectric Crystal</li><li>Pyroelectric Ceramics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pyroelectric material market is primarily divided into two types: ferroelectric crystals and pyroelectric ceramics. Ferroelectric crystals exhibit spontaneous polarization, which can be reversed by applying an external electric field, making them highly sensitive to temperature changes. Pyroelectric ceramics, on the other hand, are engineered materials that combine the properties of pyroelectricity and ferroelectricity, offering enhanced performance and versatility for applications like infrared sensors and energy harvesting. Both types are vital for various technological advancements in sensing and electronics.</p></p>
