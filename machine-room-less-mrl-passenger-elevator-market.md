<p><p>Machine Room Less (MRL) passenger elevators are designed to optimize space and energy efficiency by eliminating the need for a separate machine room. The two primary types of MRL elevators are traction and hydraulic. Traction MRL elevators use a system of ropes and pulleys for smooth vertical movement, ideal for mid to high-rise buildings. In contrast, hydraulic MRL elevators utilize a fluid-driven piston to lift the cab, making them suitable for low to mid-rise applications where speed is less critical.</p></p>
