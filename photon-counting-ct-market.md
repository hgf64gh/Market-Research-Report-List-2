<p><p>Photon Counting CT (Computed Tomography) market is divided into two main types: Single Channel Counting Systems and Multichannel Counting Systems. Single Channel Counting Systems utilize one detector to count photons, providing high sensitivity and resolution for specific applications. In contrast, Multichannel Counting Systems employ multiple detectors to capture a broader spectrum of photons simultaneously, enhancing imaging speed and efficiency. Both systems are integral to advancing diagnostic imaging capabilities, improving accuracy, and enhancing patient care in medical settings.</p></p>
