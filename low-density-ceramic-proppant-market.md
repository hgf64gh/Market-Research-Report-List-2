<p><p>The Low Density Ceramic Proppant market is categorized into Large Mesh and Small Mesh types based on particle size. Large Mesh proppants, typically with a larger diameter, are designed for high conductivity in fractures, making them suitable for deeper wells with high pressure. In contrast, Small Mesh proppants have a finer granule size, offering better packability and enhanced resistance to proppant flowback in less demanding environments. Both types are essential for optimizing oil and gas extraction efficiency.</p></p>
