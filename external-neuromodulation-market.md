<p><p>The external neuromodulation market encompasses various techniques that utilize electrical stimulation to modulate neural activity. Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation (TENS) delivers low-voltage electrical impulses through the skin to alleviate pain and improve function. Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS) utilizes magnetic fields to stimulate nerve cells in the brain, effectively treating depression and other neurological disorders. Respiratory Electrical Stimulation targets the respiratory muscles through electrical impulses, enhancing breathing control in patients with respiratory disorders. These techniques are gaining popularity for their non-invasive therapeutic benefits.</p></p>
