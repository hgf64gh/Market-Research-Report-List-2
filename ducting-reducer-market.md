<p><ul><li>Concentric Reducer</li><li>Eccentric Reducer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ducting reducer market comprises two primary types: concentric and eccentric reducers. Concentric reducers maintain a symmetrical shape, allowing smooth transitions in duct systems, ideal for vertical installations. Eccentric reducers, on the other hand, have an offset design, facilitating proper drainage and reducing vibration in horizontal systems. Both types are crucial in HVAC applications, enabling efficient airflow management and space optimization while meeting varying industrial requirements. Their distinct designs cater to specific operational needs in ventilation systems.</p></p>
