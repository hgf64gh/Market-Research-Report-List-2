<p><p>The wearable bioelectronic skin patches market encompasses several sensor types, each serving distinct health monitoring purposes. Electrophysiological sensors measure electrical signals from the body, often used for heart and muscle activity. Biochemical sensors analyze body fluids for biomarkers, aiding in disease detection. Potentiometric sensors gauge ion concentration, useful for monitoring electrolytes. Tactile sensors detect pressure and touch, enhancing interaction or feedback in medical applications. Together, these sensors enable continuous health tracking and personalized healthcare insights, driving market growth.</p></p>
