<p><p>The Commercial Wireless Fire Detection System market includes two primary types: Addressable Wireless Fire Detection Systems and Conventional Wireless Fire Detection Systems. Addressable systems use intelligent technology to identify specific locations of fire alarms, allowing for efficient monitoring and quicker response times. In contrast, Conventional systems group multiple detectors together, triggering alarms without pinpointing the exact source. Both systems enhance fire safety in commercial spaces, but addressable systems offer advanced features and greater control over fire management.</p></p>
