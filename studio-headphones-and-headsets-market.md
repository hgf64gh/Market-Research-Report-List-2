<p><p>The studio headphones and headsets market is categorized into three main types based on design: closed back, semi-open back, and fully-open back. Closed back headphones provide isolation, preventing sound leakage and ensuring focused monitoring, making them ideal for recording. Semi-open back models balance isolation and natural sound, suitable for mixing. Fully-open back headphones offer an expansive soundstage, enhancing listening experience but with less isolation, making them preferable for casual listening and sound design in quieter environments.</p></p>
