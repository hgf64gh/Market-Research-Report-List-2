<p><p>The Rubber Accelerator Masterbatch market is categorized primarily into EPDM-based, NBR-based, and Others. EPDM-based masterbatches are derived from ethylene-propylene-diene monomer rubber, providing excellent heat, oxidation, and weather resistance, making them ideal for automotive and roofing applications. NBR-based masterbatches utilize nitrile rubber, offering superior oil and fuel resistance, frequently used in automotive and industrial seals. The “Others” category encompasses various specialty formulations catering to niche markets, such as silicone or natural rubber blends, which fulfill specific performance requirements.</p></p>
