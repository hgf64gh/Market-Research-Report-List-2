<p><p>The Synthetic Surgery Adhesion Barrier Market encompasses three primary formulations: film, gel, and liquid. Film formulations provide a protective layer over surgical sites, minimizing scar tissue formation through a thin, flexible barrier. Gel formulations offer a viscous solution that adheres to tissues, reducing friction and promoting healing. Liquid formulations are applied directly to tissues in a fluid state, effectively creating a barrier against adhesions. Each type is designed to enhance surgical outcomes by preventing post-operative complications associated with tissue adhesion.</p></p>
