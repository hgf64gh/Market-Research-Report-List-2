<p><p>The electronic bird repeller market comprises various types designed to deter birds effectively. Sonic bird repellers emit loud, distressing sounds that mimic alarm calls, targeting a range of bird species. Ultrasonic bird repellers utilize high-frequency sounds that are inaudible to humans but deterring to birds, providing a discreet solution. Laser bird repellers use focused beams of light to disorient and scare birds away, creating a visually dynamic method of bird control. Each type serves distinct needs in bird management across various settings.</p></p>
