<p><p>Retail self-checkout systems can be categorized into three main types: stand-alone self-checkout systems, wall-mounted self-checkout systems, and countertop self-checkout systems. Stand-alone systems are freestanding kiosks that allow customers to scan and pay for items independently. Wall-mounted systems are integrated into store fixtures, saving floor space while providing self-service options. Countertop systems are designed for placement on counters, ideal for smaller retailers or specific checkout zones, enabling efficient transactions in limited areas. Each type enhances customer convenience and optimizes store operations.</p></p>
