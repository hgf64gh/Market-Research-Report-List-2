<p><p>The Bottle Grade PET Chips market is categorized based on intrinsic viscosity (IV), which influences the performance and application of the chips in producing PET bottles. The IV range of 0.80-0.85 denotes lower viscosity, suitable for standard bottles requiring moderate strength and clarity. In contrast, the IV range of 0.85-0.90 signifies higher viscosity, enabling enhanced mechanical properties and better barrier performance, making it ideal for high-quality, durable bottles used in specialized applications like food and beverage packaging.</p></p>
