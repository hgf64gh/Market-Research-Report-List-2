<p><p>The medical neck braces market is primarily categorized into soft and rigid neck braces. Soft neck braces offer support while allowing limited movement, often used for minor injuries or muscle strains, providing comfort and flexibility. Rigid neck braces, on the other hand, immobilize the neck completely, used for more severe injuries or post-surgery recovery, ensuring stability and protection. Both types serve critical roles in rehabilitation, pain management, and prevention of further injury, tailored to patients' specific needs.</p></p>
