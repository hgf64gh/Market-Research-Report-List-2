<p><ul><li>Spiral Overtube</li><li>Balloon Overtube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical overtube market comprises devices designed to facilitate endoscopic procedures. Spiral overtubes feature a helical design that enhances maneuverability and reduces trauma during insertion, making them ideal for navigating tight anatomical spaces. Balloon overtubes, on the other hand, utilize an inflatable element to dilate the gastrointestinal tract, easing the passage of endoscopes and instruments. Both types improve procedural efficiency, patient comfort, and safety, catering to the diverse needs of gastrointestinal diagnostics and therapeutic interventions.</p></p>
