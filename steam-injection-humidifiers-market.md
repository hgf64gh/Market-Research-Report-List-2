<p><p>Steam injection humidifiers are categorized into automatic and manual types. Automatic steam injection humidifiers utilize sensors and control systems to continuously monitor and adjust humidity levels, providing consistent performance with minimal user intervention. In contrast, manual steam injection humidifiers require user input for operation, relying on set timers or user-adjusted controls to manage humidity. Both types serve essential functions in various settings such as industrial processes, HVAC systems, and agricultural applications, ensuring optimal humidity for specific environments.</p></p>
