<p><p>The semiconductor process tapes market includes two main types: UV tape and non-UV tape. UV tape is designed for applications requiring exposure to ultraviolet light, offering excellent adhesion and easy removal without residue, making it ideal for photolithography processes. Non-UV tape, on the other hand, is utilized in various packaging and protecting applications where UV exposure is not a factor, often providing high-temperature resistance and durability. Both types cater to different manufacturing needs in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
