<p><p>The paper phenolic copper clad laminate market primarily comprises two types: single-sided copper clad and double-sided copper clad laminates. Single-sided copper clad features a copper layer on one side, making it suitable for simple circuits and applications where minimal layers are needed. In contrast, double-sided copper clad has copper on both sides, allowing for more complex circuitry and enhanced electrical performance. Both types are widely used in various electronic applications, offering unique advantages based on design requirements and manufacturing processes.</p></p>
